Taste the Rainbow

Blackcurrant

Blackcurrant

The Blackcurrant Skittle is the first on the list of the original Skittles. It is more commonly known as Kiwifruit or Grape, however in Ireland, the UK and Sweden, purple Skittles are blackcurrant.

Lemon

Lemon

The lemon Skittle is next on the list of the original skittles with its iconic yellow color. Skittles products come in a wide variety of colors, although the Skittles products often use the same colors for sweets with different flavors. An example of this is the yellow skittle. Many people associate it with lemon, however in the Tropical Skittles pack yellow Skittles are actually banana berry.

Green Apple

RIP LIME

The Green Apple Skittle is the most controversial of the skittle bunch. It was once the fan favorite lime skittle, however as of 2013 it was replaced by Green Apple in Tawian, South Korea, China, Australia, and the United States of America. Rest in peace Lime.

orange

The orange one

The Orange Skittle is one of the most diverse flavors. It takes on over 10 different other flavors in the Skittles family including Mandarin Orange, Juicy Orange, Blood Orange, Orange Creme, Orange, Mango tangelo, Sweet Orange, Twisted Tangerine, tangerine, Sour Mandarin, Blazin' Mango, Flamin' Orange, and Sour Orange.

Strawberry

The red one

The Strawberry Skittle, another fan favorite, often taking the champion spot in terms of popularity is also one of the original 6. This power ranger of a skittle is by far the most memorable color with its famous bright red color much like the first color in the rainbow, and the color of the Iconic skittles packaging.

Cherry Cola

The blue one

Cherry cola is ok. Not many people know of it. It's part of the original packaging from the late 1970s and has been included in many Tropical packs since then.

Banana Berry

Another yellow one

The Banana Berry Skittle is always the most disappointing of the Skittles. It looks like lemon, but tastes oddly fruity. Everyone knows that banana candy is always hit or miss and I think the Banana Berry Skittle is a HARD miss!

Kiwi Lime

A sad green one

Again, the green skittle does not get the justice it deserves. The green skittle is always the most underwhelming of them all. Given that the Lime flavor that everyone came to know and love back in 1974 has now been poorly replaced with a sadly sweetened version of the famous Lime skittle.

Blue Raspberry Lemon

A mixed up fella!

Stepping into the 21st century we're greeted with some crazy concoctions developed by the Skittles company. In 2008 they introduced "skittles CRAZY CORES". Each respective skittle had an outside color but a core of a complementary inside color. Blue raspberry lemon is the first on the roster and it surely has its game face on!

Sour Grape

Really upset purple!

Sour Grape is the first on the roster of the famous sour skittles branch. The best way to rip your mouth open and accurately simulate chewing on glass is to grab a bag of Sour Skittles and chow down on some sour grape boys and some of his friends!
